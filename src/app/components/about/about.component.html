About Us

The Komunidad Foundation is a non-profit organization in the Philippines dedicated to helping marginalized communities through various initiatives and programs. Our mission is to improve the lives of underserved communities by providing them with access to education, healthcare, and livelihood opportunities. The Foundation's outreach efforts focus on remote areas where basic services are often lacking, such as indigenous peoples' communities, rural villages, and urban poor settlements. Their programs include the provision of school supplies and educational support to children, medical missions. livelihood training, and community development projects. By empowering marginalized communities through their inclusive outreach efforts, the Komunidad Foundation is making a significant impact in improving the lives of those who need it most.
